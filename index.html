California Fresno Mission

About

This website is dedicated to sharing my mission experience with others. One year ago I returned home from serving an 18-month mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Throughout my time as a missionary in Fresno I developed some amazing relationships, enjoyed the California sunsets, ate great food, and became better friends with Jesus Christ. I'm excited to share this with you!

What is a missionary?

A full-mission is an opportunity that young adults have to teach people in different parts of the world about Jesus Christ. Listed below are some of the activites that a missionary participates in regularly:

  • Daily Scripture Study
  • Smile at people
  • Pray, pray, pray
  • Sweat in the heat
  • Serve in the community
  • Teach people about Jesus Christ
  • Help other missionaries
  • Pray some more

Sound cool? Yeah, it is. And better than getting to know a new place and a new culture is getting to know Jesus Christ better.

Personal Experience

I was one of the missionaries that was blessed to be able to serve during the pandemic. (For the first 9 months of my mission I didn't go to in-person Church!) As a result, we used social media to find people to share the Gospel with.


One night, my companion and I began messaging a man from a little town in the outskirts of Fresno. His name was *Pablo. Pablo soon began opening up to us about some really difficult challenges that he was facing. We got to know him over videocall and promised him that the message we shared as missionaries would change his life.


The first time we met Pablo in person was at Panda Express. Pablo was so nervous; he had never spent much time with God before. We started with the basics and taught him how to pray. He had never prayed before. It was uncomfortable for him and he mumbled and stumbled over his words. It was the simplest and shortest prayer I have ever heard, but it was full of yearning to know of God's existence. That night, Panda Express was a holy place for us.


One month after his first uttered prayer, Pablo was baptized. Pablo's journey hasn't been easy, but he remains dedicated to Jesus Christ and Heavenly Father. The biggest lesson I have learned from Pablo is the power of sincere prayer.
